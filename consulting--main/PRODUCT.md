# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Founders, CEOs, Product Managers, and Senior Leaders aiming to transition from operational management to strategic advisory and C-suite influence. High-potential candidates preparing for MBB (McKinsey, BCG, Bain) or Big 4 strategy roles.

## Product Purpose
A 5-week intensive programme ("Beyond Career") designed to decode the logic of global strategy firms, master structural architecture, quantitative precision, and executive narrative strategy. Success is defined by candidates securing elite consulting offers or elevated executive presence.

## Brand Personality
Modern Technical, Elite, Strategic, Confident.
(Note: Shifting from Editorial to a more precise, high-performance technical aesthetic like Linear or Stripe).

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS landing page templates, "AI-slop" (over-saturated gradients, generic card grids, excessive decorative blurs), corporate clinical blue, and low-information marketing fluff.

## Design Principles
1. **Structural Logic**: The UI itself should feel "MECE" (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive)—organized, logical, and easy to deconstruct mentally.
2. **Quantitative Precision**: Use precise spacing, crisp borders, and monospaced accents where appropriate to reflect analytical rigor.
3. **High-Stakes Authority**: Design for a "Partner-level" review; every pixel should be defensible and intentional, avoiding decorative noise.
4. **Frictionless Sophistication**: High-performance interactions (smooth scroll, precise motion) that feel effortless and "expensive" without being over-animated.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for text contrast and interactive targets.
- Support for reduced motion (respect `prefers-reduced-motion`).
- Clear focus states for keyboard navigation (essential for power users).
